Eaton Centre Stores

Have fun shopping in the biggest mall in Toronto!


This mall has five levels, the main level being level 2. In level 2, the biggest store is Hudson's Bay and Sax's Fifth Avenue. The joint store is known for selling high quality clothing, brands from Levi's to Fendi. If you have a big wallet, you might spend a lot of time in these stores. However, there are many other stores in this level that are designer brands like Armani Exchange. This is the perfect floor if you are just looking around to buy something unique. Level 1 consist of common stores like Forever 21 and Foot Locker. Level 3 is also a unique floor, and has stores such as OVO, AllSaints, and Williams-Sonoma. Level 1 is mainly for common and child clothing. It also contains other service stores like cellular service and salons. There are also vendors in the middle for small companies. The Urban Eatery is where the food court is located. There are common food stores such as McDonald's and Tim Hortons.
